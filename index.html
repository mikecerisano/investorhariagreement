MIMIC INVESTOR AGREEMENT

1. Investment & Ownership Terms

  • 1.1 The Investor is contributing $15,000 in exchange for 20% ownership in the short film MIMIC.
  • 1.2 Ownership applies only to the short film MIMIC. It does not extend to other productions by Red Fox Features LLC.
  • 1.3 Any revenue generated from MIMIC after production and post-production expenses have been fully paid will first go toward recouping:
    • 1.3.1 Investor contributions.
    • 1.3.2 Any additional funds contributed by the team.
    • 1.3.3 After full recoupment, any net profit will be distributed among ownership stakeholders proportionally based on ownership percentage.

2. Investor Benefits & Participation

2.1 Recognition & Credit

  • 2.1.1 Executive Producer Credit – Investor will be credited as an Executive Producer in the film's credits.
  • 2.1.2 IMDb Credit – Investor will receive an official IMDb listing as an Executive Producer.

2.2 Exclusive Access & Events

  • 2.2.1 Private Screening – Investor will be invited to a private screening before the festival run, if one is held.
  • 2.2.2 Festival Invitations – Investor will receive invitations to festival premieres and industry events where MIMIC is screened.

2.3 Memorabilia & Production Perks

  • 2.3.1 Signed Memorabilia – Investor will receive a signed script, poster, or props from the production as a thank-you.
  • 2.3.2 Behind-the-Scenes Content – Investor will receive exclusive behind-the-scenes footage or a documentary about the making of the film, if one is produced.
  • 2.3.3 Trophy (If Permitted by Festival) – If the festival allows for additional awards or trophies to be produced, the investor will receive a customized trophy with their Executive Producer credit.

3. Posting & Distribution Restrictions

  • 3.1 Investor will be given a private link to view the film once completed. However, the investor may not publicly post or share the film until approved by the production team.
  • 3.2 Posting the full film publicly before the festival run may result in disqualification from certain festivals.
  • 3.3 If the film secures a distribution deal (e.g., on Dust, Shudder, or other platforms), the investor must comply with the terms of that deal and cannot re-host the film elsewhere.
  • 3.4 The production team will provide the investor with approved promotional materials (trailers, posters, teasers) to share instead of the full film before public release.
  • 3.5 If the investor wishes to share the film privately with specific individuals, they must inform the production team in advance to prevent any accidental public leaks that could affect festival eligibility.

4. Legal Terms & Dispute Resolution

4.1 No Guaranteed Returns

  • 4.1.1 The Investor understands that independent film investment is speculative, and returns are not guaranteed.

4.2 Creative Control

  • 4.2.1 Investor acknowledges that all creative decisions, casting, and production choices remain solely with Red Fox Features LLC and its agreements within MIMIC's creative team, though investor input is welcomed.

4.3 Force Majeure Clause

  • 4.3.1 If unforeseen circumstances (natural disasters, pandemics, strikes, etc.) delay production, the Production Company will make best efforts to complete the film within a reasonable timeframe.

4.4 Governing Law & Arbitration

  • 4.4.1 State Jurisdiction – This agreement is governed by the laws of Delaware (where Red Fox Features LLC is registered).
  • 4.4.2 Dispute Resolution – Any disputes shall be settled through arbitration under Delaware law.
  • 4.4.3 Modifications – Any changes to this agreement must be made in writing and agreed upon by both parties.

5. Payment Terms

  • 5.1 The $15,000 investment is due upon signing of this agreement and must be transferred via ACH, wire transfer, or another mutually agreed-upon method to the designated Red Fox Features LLC business account.
